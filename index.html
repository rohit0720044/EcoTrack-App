Eco goals and challenges

Build small habits that stick

Climate fact

Electricity choices matter

Efficient appliances, switched-off standby devices, and renewable energy plans can reduce household emissions quickly.

Daily habit

Short trips are opportunities

Walking, cycling, public transport, or carpooling for nearby travel can lower transport emissions while improving routine fitness.

Sustainable living

Reuse before recycling

Repairing, sharing, refilling, and buying local products often avoids emissions before waste management even begins.
